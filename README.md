## 🧭 About Me

I'm a Computer Science student at Prince Sultan University, majoring in **Cybersecurity**, with a hands-on focus on network security, incident response, and risk management. I'm currently a **Full-Stack Developer co-op** at Aljdwa, building web applications with **React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and PHP** — pairing that development work with a security-first mindset shaped by my ISC² coursework.

I approach engineering the way I approach security: methodically. I care about writing software that's not just functional, but defensible — clean architecture, secure coding practices (Secure SDLC), and attention to how systems fail before they fail in production. I'm actively working toward **CCNA**, **CEH**, and **CompTIA Network+** to deepen my technical foundation on the security side while continuing to grow as a full-stack engineer.
